## Discord

Discord is Orax's original and primary platform. Invite the Discord bot to your server to start syncing channels, reactions, threads, stickers, and polls across multiple servers.

[**Invite Orax for Discord →**](https://discord.com/api/oauth2/authorize?client_id=812298057470967858&permissions=533113138240&scope=bot)

## Fluxer

Fluxer is a growing chat platform supported by Orax. Invite the Fluxer bot to your server to bring the same cross-server bridging experience to your Fluxer community.

[**Invite Orax for Fluxer →**](https://web.fluxer.app/oauth2/authorize?client_id=1527278943076352000&scope=bot&permissions=2251937790028864)

<Callout type="info">
  Channels on Discord and Fluxer can be linked to the same interserv group, letting
  users on different platforms chat together seamlessly.
</Callout>
