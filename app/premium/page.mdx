# Orax Plus

import { Callout, Table } from "nextra/components";

Orax Plus is the premium tier that unlocks higher limits and supports the ongoing development of the bot. It can be obtained by **voting** (free, temporary — Top.gg for Discord servers, Fluxerlist for Fluxer servers), by **subscribing monthly via Stripe** ($2.99/month), or by purchasing a **lifetime plan** ($19.99 once).

## Plan Comparison

| Feature                    | Free | Orax Plus                                       |
| -------------------------- | ---- | ----------------------------------------------- |
| **Max groups per server**  | 2    | 100                                             |
| **Max channels per group** | 5    | 50                                              |
| **All features**           | ✅   | ✅                                              |
| **Auto Translate**         | ❌   | ✅                                              |
| **Price**                  | $0   | $2.99/month, $19.99 lifetime, or free via voting |

<Callout type="info">
  Orax Plus unlocks **Auto Translate** (automatic message translation across
  interserv channels) in addition to higher limits on groups and channels. All
  other features are available on the free plan.
</Callout>

## Getting Orax Plus

There are three ways to get Orax Plus:

### Option 1: Vote (Free)

Vote for Orax on the bot listing for your platform. Each vote grants Orax Plus for approximately **one week**. You can vote again to extend the duration.

- **Cost**: Free
- **Duration**: ~1 week per vote
- **Activation**: Automatic
- **Limits**: Same as paid Orax Plus
- **Discord servers**: Vote on [Top.gg](https://top.gg/bot/812298057470967858/vote)
- **Fluxer servers**: Vote on [Fluxerlist](https://fluxerlist.com/bots/orax)

See [Vote for Orax Plus](/premium/vote) for detailed instructions.

### Option 2: Monthly subscription ($2.99/month)

Subscribe to Orax Plus via Stripe for a recurring monthly subscription.

- **Cost**: $2.99/month
- **Duration**: Active as long as subscription is active
- **Activation**: Immediate upon successful payment
- **Cancellation**: Cancel anytime from the Stripe customer portal

### Option 3: Lifetime ($19.99 once)

Pay once and keep Orax Plus forever for the selected server.

- **Cost**: $19.99 (one-time)
- **Duration**: Lifetime of the server
- **Activation**: Immediate upon successful payment
- **No recurring charges**

<Callout type="tip">
  Voting is completely free and gives you the same benefits as the paid
  subscription. If you can't afford the subscription, voting is a great
  alternative — it's available on both Discord (Top.gg) and Fluxer
  (Fluxerlist).
</Callout>

## Managing Your Subscription

### Checking Your Status

You can check your Orax Plus status from:

- The [Orax Dashboard](https://oraxbot.com/dashboard) — the Orax Plus panel shows your current status and expiry
- The dashboard API (`/v2/get_orax_plus_status` endpoint)

### Cancellation

Stripe monthly subscriptions can be cancelled at any time. Upon cancellation:

- Orax Plus remains active until the end of the current billing period
- After the billing period ends, limits revert to the free tier
- Existing groups and channels are **not** deleted — you just can't create new ones beyond the free limits

Lifetime plans do not require cancellation — they remain active permanently.

## Data and Privacy

Orax Plus entitlements are stored in the database with the following information:

- Guild ID
- Source (`stripe`, `stripe_lifetime`, `topgg_vote`, `fluxerlist_vote`, or `manual`)
- User ID (who activated it)
- Start and end dates
- Status (active/inactive)

No payment details are stored by Orax — all payment processing is handled securely by Stripe.
